Welcome

My name is Raelyn, welcome to my personal writing portfolio.

In my tiny corner of the internet, I hope you enjoy my words and general expression of creativity. I’ve laughed through writing them, cried over them, and maybe even questioned my existence over them, but you’ll never know if that’s true or not. This is a little glimpse into what my brain looks & sounds like through tiny internet pixels.

My words are what make me into the person I am and continue to transform me into the person I want to become. Music is what drives me to create, and soothes the deepest cuts. Every situation and every moment, to me, is matched with a song, an album, or string of melodies. Each of these pictures were taken by me, and each has an album associated with that time or feeling.

Choose a picture/album wisely, and enjoy your reading experience, curated by me.
